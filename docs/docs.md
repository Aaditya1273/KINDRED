The Product: "KINDRED" (Kinetic Intelligence & Networked Decentralized REal-time Data)
KINDRED is the world’s first Autonomous Personal Hedge Fund & Data Sovereign. It is a mobile-first "Consumer DeFi" agent that manages your entire digital and financial life while you sleep.

How it wins every track:
1. Flow (Consumer DeFi - 1st Place): KINDRED uses Flow's account abstraction. Users sign up with an Email/Passkey. The AI manages "Scheduled Savings" and "Auto-Yield" loops. It feels like a high-yield savings account, but under the hood, it’s a complex agentic DeFi strategy.

2. Fresh Code (1st Place): This is a ground-up architecture utilizing the new ERC-8004 agent standard.

3. Zama (Confidential Finance): KINDRED uses FHE (Fully Homomorphic Encryption) to analyze your private spending habits and bank statements to give you Alpha/DeFi advice without ever seeing your actual data.

4. Filecoin/Storacha: The AI’s "Long-term Memory" and "Strategy Logs" are stored on Filecoin/Storacha. It’s an agent that never forgets and is completely portable.

5. World ID: To prevent bot-farming, every KINDRED agent is tethered to a World ID. One human, one elite financial agent.

6. Lit Protocol: Uses Lit for "Programmable Signing"—the agent can trade on your behalf only when specific market conditions (signed by an oracle) are met.




















Part 3: The Production-Level Product Blueprint
The "Killer Feature": The Blind Wealth Manager
Imagine an app where you upload your private financial history. The AI (running on Zama/FHE) processes that data while it is still encrypted. It realizes you are overpaying for insurance and under-yielding on your savings. It then automatically moves your USDC on Flow to a higher-yield vault using a Scheduled Transaction.













Part 4: How to execute to "Beat them Brutally"
To secure 1st place, your submission must not look like a hackathon project. It must look like a Seed Round Series A startup.

The "Agent Manifest" (agent.json): Most teams will ignore this. You will make yours perfect. Define your agent's capabilities, tools, and "Safety Guardrails" explicitly.

The "Receipts" (agent_log.json): Your demo video shouldn't just show a UI; it should show the Execution Logs. Prove the agent made a decision, verified it on-chain (Starknet/Filecoin), and executed it on Flow.

The Visuals: High-fidelity UI. Use a "Consumer Fintech" aesthetic (Dark mode, glassmorphism, 0 crypto-jargon). Call it "Smart Cash," not "Yield Aggregator."

The "Wow" Factor: During the demo, show the agent self-correcting. "I attempted to swap on Flow, but slippage was too high, so I moved the task to a scheduled window 2 hours from now." This proves Autonomy.
